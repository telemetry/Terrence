TypeParis profile ↗

Airlie

A six-week intensive programme in Paris covering typeface design, calligraphy techniques, type history, and modern software practices. Studied under the French master type designer Jean François Porchez using the same techniques established by Adrian Frutiger in the 1950s. The outcome: Airlie - a serif family inspired by Garamond and 18th-century punch-cut forms, with softer serifs and playful curves across 3 styles and 15 weights.
