Terrence Gillespie

Principal Design Engineer / Brisbane

Dear Wallet & Apple Pay Design Team,

I design payments experiences on Apple platforms. As Principal Design Engineer at Westpac, one of Australia's four major banks, I lead native design systems and component libraries across iOS, Android, and watchOS. Bringing that work to Wallet & Apple Pay is the role I've been working toward. I've used a Mac since 1996, collect Macintosh classics, and have spent my career building on the platforms Apple makes possible.

My path here is direct. I ran my own design studio in Sydney — working in Sketch for a decade — then led mobile UX at Telstra, Australia's largest telco, then trained at the Apple Developer Academy in Naples, where I studied Pixar's storytelling methodology alongside SwiftUI and Challenge Based Learning. Today I both design and ship: tokens, components, and interaction patterns across iOS, Android, macOS, and watchOS — including Storybook-style component libraries and UI builders for native platforms. I prototype in SwiftUI, review code with our iOS team, and treat handoff as a design problem in itself. Recent work includes SafeCall and SafeBlock, scam prevention features that protect customers at the moment they pay, and leading the design integration of a personal finance app Westpac acquired. The Westpac app has been rated best in class by Forrester three years running. I present the annual design review to an engineering audience of 4,000 at TechX — building the case for craft at the scale where it's hardest to maintain.

Typography is my passion. I graduated from TypeParis last year with distinction and released a typeface, Airlie. That attention to craft and detail carries into my UI work: consistency, colour, rhythm, scale and intent.

Payments design carries a duty of care. I focus on financial literacy, the pause points that protect people from scams & costly mistakes, and accessibility built in rather than bolted on. I work best in collaborative, diverse environments — self-directed, moving fast together, and accountable to the quality of what ships. Wallet & Apple Pay reaches billions, and I'd bring that same duty to every decision. My portfolio is at terrygillespie.com/product-designer.

I'm based in Brisbane and ready to relocate to Cupertino for this role. Thank you for considering me.

Terrence Gillespie
